### 00:30 – The Tax Problem (1:30)

**[VISUAL: Người đi làm, máy tính hiện bảng thuế]**

**VO:** To understand why salary sacrifice is so powerful, you first need to understand how income tax works in Australia.

**[VISUAL: Bảng thuế thu nhập 2025-2026]**

**VO:** In 2026, the tax rates for Australian residents look like this:
- $0 to $18,200: tax-free
- $18,201 to $45,000: 16 cents per dollar
- $45,001 to $135,000: 30 cents per dollar
- $135,001 to $190,000: 37 cents per dollar
- Over $190,000: 45 cents per dollar

**[VISUAL: Người kiếm $100K — minh hoạ từng phần thu nhập bị đánh thuế]**

**VO:** Plus, you pay a 2% Medicare Levy on top. So if you earn $100,000, your marginal tax rate is 32% — that's 30% income tax plus 2% Medicare.

**[VISUAL: $100 → chỉ còn $68 sau thuế]**

**VO:** For every extra dollar you earn above $45,000, the government takes 32 cents. You keep 68.

**[VISUAL: Chia đôi màn hình — bên trái: lương thường, bên phải: salary sacrifice vào super]**

**[VISUAL: $100 lương → $15 thuế super (15%) → $85 vào tài khoản super]**

**VO:** Instead of paying 32% tax, you pay only 15% contributions tax inside super. That $100 becomes $85 working for your retirement.

**[VISUAL: So sánh trực tiếp: $68 take-home vs $85 into super]**

**VO:** Compare: $100 earned normally → you get $68 in your pocket. Same $100 salary sacrificed → $85 goes into your super. That's $17 more, every time.

**[VISUAL: Biểu đồ compound — $85 tăng trưởng 7%/năm vs $68 không đầu tư]**

**VO:** And because that $85 is invested inside super, it grows at 6-8% per year tax-efficiently. Over 30 years, the difference between $68 and $85 per $100 compounds into hundreds of thousands of dollars.

### 05:30 – The Caps: How Much Can You Sacrifice? (1:00)

**[VISUAL: Đồng hồ báo thức + chữ "LIMITS"]**

**VO:** But you can't salary sacrifice unlimited amounts. The government sets annual caps.

**[VISUAL: Con số $30,000 hiện ra]**

**VO:** In 2026, the concessional contributions cap — which includes your employer's 11.5% super guarantee plus any salary sacrifice — is $30,000 per year.

**[VISUAL: Tính toán — $95K × 11.5% = $10,925 employer → còn $19,075 cho salary sacrifice]**

**VO:** So if Sarah's employer already puts in $10,925, she can salary sacrifice up to $19,075 more before hitting the cap.

**[VISUAL: Cảnh báo — vượt cap bị phạt]**

**VO:** Go over the cap, and you pay excess contributions tax at your marginal rate plus an interest charge. So know your numbers.

**[VISUAL: Carry-forward rule — unused caps từ 5 năm trước]**

**VO:** But here's a secret: if you didn't use your full cap in previous years, you can carry forward unused amounts for up to 5 years. That means someone with a total super balance under $500,000 could potentially contribute up to $150,000 in a single year.

### 06:30 – How to Set Up Salary Sacrifice (1:00)

**[VISUAL: Màn hình HR portal / email mẫu]**

**VO:** So how do you actually set this up? It's surprisingly simple.

**[VISUAL: Step 1 — Email to HR / Payroll]**

**VO:** Step one: Write to your employer's payroll department. Just a simple email: "I'd like to salary sacrifice X dollars per pay period into my super fund."

**[VISUAL: Step 2 — Choose the amount]**

**VO:** Step two: Choose your amount. Most people start with $100-$200 per week. Remember, you're reducing your take-home pay — make sure you can afford it.

**[VISUAL: Step 3 — It happens automatically]**

**VO:** Step three: Done. Your employer handles everything. The money goes into your super before tax is calculated. You don't have to do anything else.

**[VISUAL: TIP — check super fund sau 2-4 tuần]**

**VO:** Pro tip: check your super account after 2-4 weeks to make sure the contributions are showing up. Mistakes happen.

### 07:30 – Common Myths & Pitfalls (1:30)

**[VISUAL: Myth #1 — "I can't afford it"]**

**VO:** Myth one: "I can't afford to sacrifice money." Reality: if you earn over $45,000, sacrificing $50 a week only reduces your take-home pay by $34. The rest is tax you would have paid anyway.

**[VISUAL: Tính toán — $50 → chỉ mất $34 trong túi]**

**VO:** You don't feel $34 a week. But that $50 growing at 7% for 30 years becomes $5,200. Per week.

**[VISUAL: Myth #2 — "I need the money now"]**

**VO:** Myth two: "I need the money now." Fair point — super is locked until preservation age. But here's the thing: what if you salary sacrifice just 1-2% of your salary? That's barely noticeable in your pay, but transformative in retirement.

**[VISUAL: Myth #3 — "My employer won't let me"]**

**VO:** Myth three: "My employer won't let me." Actually, by law, all employers MUST offer salary sacrifice to all employees if requested. It's not optional for them.

**[VISUAL: Warning — Centrelink + super]**

**VO:** One real caution: if you receive Centrelink benefits or have a low income, salary sacrifice might reduce your eligibility. Talk to a financial advisor or check with Centrelink first.
