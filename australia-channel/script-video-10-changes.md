# Superannuation Changes 2026-2027: Everything New

**Độ dài:** ~8 phút | **Series:** Superannuation Explained (Video #10)

**[VISUAL: Calendar 2026-2027 with Australian government stamps, new rules documents]**
**VO:** Superannuation rules change every year. If you're not keeping up, you could miss opportunities or worse — accidentally break the rules. Here's everything new for 2026-2027.

**[VISUAL: SG rate rising from 11.5% to 12% in 2028, confirmed]**
**VO:** The Super Guarantee rises to 12% by 2028. In 2026 it's 11.5%. That extra 0.5% might not sound like much, but on a $100K salary, that's $500 more into your super every year, compounding for decades.

**[VISUAL: Concessional cap $30,000, indexed to AWOTE growth]**
**VO:** The concessional contributions cap remains $30,000 for 2026-27. But it's now indexed to Average Weekly Ordinary Time Earnings, meaning it will rise automatically with wages.

**[VISUAL: Non-concessional cap $120,000 or $360,000 under 3-year bring-forward]**
**VO:** The non-concessional cap is $120,000 per year. Or $360,000 over three years using the bring-forward rule — useful if you're getting a late start on super.

**[VISUAL: First Home Super Saver Scheme increased to $60,000]**
**VO:** Big change: the First Home Super Saver Scheme withdrawable amount increased to $60,000 per person. Couples can withdraw $120,000 combined. This is a game-changer for first home buyers.

**[VISUAL: Downsizer contribution age reduced to 55]**
**VO:** The downsizer contribution age dropped to 55. Sell your family home, put up to $300,000 per person ($600K per couple) into super tax-free. Perfect for empty nesters.

**[VISUAL: Payday super - employers must pay super on payday from July 2026]**
**VO:** From July 2026, employers must pay super at the same time as salary — not quarterly. This means your super gets invested sooner and grows faster. It also makes it harder for employers to miss payments.

**[VISUAL: ATO enhanced compliance - real-time data matching, penalty increases]**
**VO:** The ATO is getting tougher. Real-time data matching means unpaid super is caught faster. Penalties for employers who don't pay on time have increased.

**[VISUAL: Performance test expanded to all super products]**
**VO:** The YourSuper performance test now covers all super products, not just MySuper defaults. Underperformers get publicly flagged.

**[VISUAL: Summary card with all 7 key changes]**
**VO:** Seven key changes to remember. SG up to 12% by 2028. Caps indexed to wages. FHSSS up to $60K. Downsizer at 55. Payday super from July. ATO enforcement tightening. Performance test expanded.
